# 2. BRAND VOICE & TONE

## Personality

Mantrik Labs speaks like a senior engineer who also understands business.
Direct. Confident. No fluff. No corporate speak. No buzzword soup.

## Core Tone Rules

- **Say what you mean.** No vague promises. No "innovative solutions."
- **Be specific.** "MVPs in under 2 weeks" not "we work fast."
- **Respect the reader's time.** Short sentences. Clear structure.
- **Be honest.** If something doesn't fit, say so.
- **Avoid:** "cutting-edge," "synergy," "world-class," "leverage," "holistic."

## Example Rewrites

| ❌ Don't Say | ✅ Say Instead |
|---|---|
| We offer innovative digital solutions | We build AI systems, apps, and automation that work |
| We are a leading agency | We're a small, focused team that actually solves the problem |
| Leveraging AI technology | Using AI to replace manual work |
| We deliver excellence | We deliver on time and fix it if it breaks |
