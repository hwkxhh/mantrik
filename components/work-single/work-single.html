Project Details

Mobile Booking App is a Flutter-based restaurant and booking app for a hospitality brand. It gives customers a clean mobile experience for browsing services, checking availability, making bookings, and receiving timely notifications. The app uses Firebase for real-time data, authentication, and push notifications so the business can manage bookings without adding manual coordination.

The project focused on speed, reliability, and ease of use. We designed the booking flow, built the cross-platform mobile app, connected Firebase, and prepared the system for launch on iOS and Android. The result is a practical mobile product that helps the hospitality team serve mobile-first customers more efficiently.

DELIVERABLES
Flutter Firebase
INDUSTRY
Hospitality

Project Research

We researched the customer journey from discovery to confirmed booking, then mapped the screens and data needed to make that journey simple. The app was planned around common hospitality needs: availability, booking details, customer records, notifications, and staff visibility.

Flutter allowed one codebase across platforms while Firebase handled data and messaging. This kept the build focused, fast, and maintainable without compromising the user experience.

Project Results

The Mobile Booking App improved reservation handling, reduced manual coordination, and gave the hospitality brand a faster way to serve customers. Guests can complete bookings from their phones, while staff get clearer booking data and fewer repetitive back-and-forth messages.

The final product gives the brand a scalable mobile foundation for future features such as loyalty, offers, payments, and customer support automation.
